About Us

History

Started in 2020,Kibia Restaurant quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in Hong Kong. With its unique brand of world fusion cuisine that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Hong Kong. Featuring four of the best three-star Michelin chefs in the world, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Frying Pan, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Sancrist Mamboleo, that featured for the first time the world's best cuisines in a pan.

Corporate Leadership

Peter Pan

CEO: Chief Executive Officer

Our CEO, Sncrist Mamboleo, credits his hardworking parents. His mother's wizardy in the kitchen whipping up the tastiest dishes with whatever is available inexpensively at the supermarket, was his first inspiration to create the fusion cuisines for which The Frying Pan became well known. He brings his zeal for fusion cuisines to this restaurant, pioneering cross-cultural culinary connections.

Danny

CFO: Chief Food Officer

Our CFO, Kibia, as she is affectionately referred to by her colleagues, comes from a long established family tradition in farming and produce. Her experiences growing up on a farm in the Australian outback gave her great appreciation for varieties of food sources. As she puts it in his own words, Everything that runs, wins, and everything that stays, pays!

samson

CTO: Chief Taste Officerr

Blessed with the most discerning gustatory sense, samson, our CTO, personally ensures that every dish that we serve meets his exacting tastes. Our chefs dread the tongue lashing that ensues if their dish does not meet his exacting standards. He lives by his motto, You click only if you survive my lick.

Titus

Executive Chef

Award winning three-star Kenyan chef with wide International experience having worked closely with whos-who in the culinary world, he specializes in creating mouthwatering Indo-Italian fusion experiences.
